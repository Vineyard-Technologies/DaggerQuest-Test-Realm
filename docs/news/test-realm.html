new logo and test realm

We're excited to announce two new updates: A new logo, and the Test Realm!

new logo

DaggerQuest has a new logo! It's not very different from the previous version. It's already on the website, and it will be added to the game in a future release.

test realm

DaggerQuest now has a Test Realm, which can be accessed at Test.DaggerQuest.com. Releases will incubate here for a while before going to DaggerQuest.com. The contents of the Test Realm may reset periodically, and you may encounter bugs or incomplete features.
